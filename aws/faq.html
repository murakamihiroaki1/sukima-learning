About the Service

What is this service?
A study support service for those aiming to pass AWS certifications. It helps you study efficiently with practical question sets and detailed explanations.
Which certifications are supported?
The following certifications are currently available:
  • Foundational
    • CLF - AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02)
    • AIF - AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01)
  • Associate
    • SAA - AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03)
    • MLA - AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer - Associate (MLA-C01) ※Coming soon
    • SOA - AWS Certified SysOps Administrator - Associate (SOA-C02) ※Coming soon
    • DEA - AWS Certified Data Engineer - Associate (DEA-C01) ※Coming soon
    • DVA - AWS Certified Developer - Associate (DVA-C02) ※Coming soon
  • Professional
    • Planned for future release

Pricing & Plans

What can I access on the free plan?
The free plan gives you access to sample questions (20 questions). We recommend a paid plan for serious exam preparation.
What payment methods are accepted?
We plan to accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB, American Express).
What are the pricing plans?
The Standard plan is ¥980/month. The Advanced plan is ¥1,980/month (coming soon).

Study Features

What is category-based study?
A feature that lets you filter questions by AWS service category (e.g. Compute, Storage, Database). Available on the Standard plan or higher.
What is mock exam mode?
A mode that lets you practice in the same format and time limits as the real exam — ideal for assessing your readiness. Available on the Standard plan or higher.

Technical Questions

Can I use this on a smartphone?
Yes! It works on smartphones, tablets, and PCs. The responsive design ensures an optimal experience on any device.
